Picao Caldense - Société des sauces

Picao Caldense - Société des sauces is crafted in small batches, with a strong emphasis on care, flexibility, and authenticity. While rooted in traditional Colombian flavor, each batch is made with the understanding that every kitchen and every customer has its own way of enjoying food.

This is why customization is an important part of what we do. From adjusting spice levels to tailoring heat, texture, or batch size, Picao Caldense is designed to adapt without losing its identity. The goal is not to standardize flavor, but to make it personal — so each bottle feels like it belongs in your home, your table, and your way of cooking.

Whether it is a milder version for everyday meals, a stronger heat profile for those who prefer intensity, or custom quantities for families, events, or food service, each request is handled with the same attention to detail and respect for the craft.

Every variation still carries the same foundation: slow-building spice, balanced seasoning, and the comforting depth of homemade Colombian tradition. Customization does not change the soul of the product — it brings it closer to the people who enjoy it.
