Heritage

A story of family, tradition, and quiet craftsmanship behind every flavor—where the recipe carries the memory of those who came before us and the care of those who continue it today.

Rooted in Tradition

Picao Caldense is deeply rooted in Colombian culinary culture, where food is far more than sustenance—it is identity, memory, and celebration woven into everyday life. Meals are shared stories that connect generations, preserve heritage, and honor the simple yet meaningful act of gathering around a table. Each flavor reflects a lineage of tradition passed down with care, shaped by time, place, and the hands of family who cooked not from instruction, but from memory.

Craft & Respect

Our approach is guided by deep respect—for ingredients, for process, and for the traditions that shaped them. We believe that true flavor cannot be rushed. It is built slowly, through patience, repetition, and intention, in kitchens where technique was learned through observation and experience rather than written instruction. Every step reflects the discipline of traditional home cooking passed down through generations, where nothing is wasted and everything carries purpose.

Our Values

We are guided by authenticity, consistency, and cultural pride. Authenticity in staying true to our origins without compromise. Consistency in delivering depth, balance, and quality in every bottle. Cultural pride in honoring the Colombian heritage that defines who we are. These values are not abstract ideas—they are practices embedded in every decision we make, shaping every bottle we produce.

Société des sauces

More than a brand, Picao Caldense is a living expression of Colombian flavor—refined through time, crafted with intention, and made to be shared, remembered, and felt across every table it reaches.
