Our Story

Salsa Picao Caldense comes from a culture where food carries history. In Colombian homes, recipes are not written as instructions — they are passed through experience, memory, and love.

This salsa was created to preserve that feeling. Not as something modern or distant, but as something familiar — rooted in tradition and carried forward through flavor.
